## General Tips

1. Have a good rest (especially the night)
  before long trips (especially road trips)
  .

2. Check Google map and get familiar about the route for road trips.

3. Download Google offline maps.

## Travel to US with B1/B2 Visa

Make sure to update [EVUS](https://www.evus.gov/evus/#/)
information within 72 hours before entering the US.

## Weather 

Check weather including the starting city and destination cities.
