## Separate 2G and 5G WiFi Networks

1. Go to https://internet.xfinity.com/

1. Click on the name of your WiFi.

1. Click "Edit WiFi" in the upper-left corner.

1. Check the checkbox "Use different names and passwords" in the prompt.
