## Backup Care

Many companies provides backup care as one of their benefits.
Typically,
backup care works with care.com to hire baby sitters.
However,
you can even hire people that you trust (e..g, friends or family) to take care of your kids and then ask for reimbursement on care.com. 
Some companies might limit how many reimbursement an employee can have quarterly and yeartly though.
