## Tips

1. [Adjust volume of different audio sources](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U87t9udE5Xw&t=92s)
  .

2. [Adjust wiper sensitivity level](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U87t9udE5Xw&t=485s)
  .

3. Adaptive high beam assist.
    Settings -> Vehicle -> Displays and Lighting -> High Beam Assist.
    Select on "Adaptive".

1. [Set up storage for Gear Guard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ND5CrNOoJMk)
    .
    The maximum support disk is 2T
    and the required minimum disk is 128G. 
    I personally recommended using 1T or 2T disks.

2. [Enhanced Highway Assist](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r5iX7ZMiRnY)
  . 
  It only works on pre-mapped highways.

3. [Camping model](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BrpBU69ijig)
    which supports 
    [auto leveling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BrpBU69ijig&t=170s)
    .
    
4. Rivian comes with a 
    [torch / flashlight](https://www.youtube.com/watch?app=desktop&v=dciR0cow3ys&t=30s)
    which is integrated into the driver's side door.

4. [Pet comfort mode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KIvn9NvddlM&t=139s)
    .

5. Garage door pairing. 

6. [Air compressor](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zyn5lRaPefI)
    . 

7. Rivian has a built-in navigation system (which is based on Google Map). 
    However,
    it is often more convenient to explore locations and routes on your phone.
    Rivian allows any driver on a Rivian car to directly share Google Map from their phone to the Rivian app
    to open the route using the built-in navigation system.

8. Memorizing AC vent positions. 

9. Disable unlocking at home.
    See 
    [Proximity Locking on Rivian]( https://www.legendu.net/misc/blog/proximity-locking-on-Rivian ) 
    for discussion.

10. Rivian supports 
    [auto kneeling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaBvLCuAPhg)
    which makes it convenient for children and small people 
    to hop on/off the car.
    However,
    the behavior of auto kneeling is not consistently
    especially when it's interruptted by opening door when parked.
    I'd suggest you disable (default) this feature.

11. Rivian Connect+. Once subscribed, you can use its WiFi hotspot. 

12. Rivian R1S does not support adjusting the height of the liftgate currently.
    However,
    there are walkaround as discussed in 
    [Rivian R1S Liftgate Height Adjustment Possible?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=of3k7Icyzgg)
    .

13. [Capturing vehicle diagnostics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U87t9udE5Xw&t=443s)
    .
  
14. [Car wash mode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KIvn9NvddlM&t=226s)
    .
    
15. Roadside lighting
