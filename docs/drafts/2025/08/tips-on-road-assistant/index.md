## General Tips

1. Some car manufactors provide roadside assistant. 
    For example,
    Rivian provides 24/7 roadside assistance 
    for Rivian vehicles for the duration of the New Vehicle Limited Warranty.
    You can access Roadside Assistance 24/7 through the Rivian app and center display.

2. Insurance companies allow car insurance policies to purchase roadside assistant.
    Be aware of whether roadside assistant is include in your car insurance policy.

3. Some credit cards (e.g., BOA) provide roadside assistant as a benefit.
