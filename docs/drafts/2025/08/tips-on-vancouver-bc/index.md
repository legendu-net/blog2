## Credit Card

Use Google Wallet Pay and/or Apple Pay if possible.
  At least, you should use credit cards with good fraud protection
  (e.g., Chase).

## EV Charging

The Tesla Supercharger
at "4660 Garden City Rd, Richmond" 
is a good one.

1. 40 chargers
2. V3 superchargers and thus open to third-party EVs
3. good charging power (250kw)
4. food & shopping nearby

## Vancouver Aquarium

There are 2 level-2 chargers in front of the entrance to Vancouver Aquarium,
which is a huge advantage for EVs
as parking spots can be hard to find.

## Stanley Park

1. Paid parking session is valid at any parking lot in Stanley Park.
