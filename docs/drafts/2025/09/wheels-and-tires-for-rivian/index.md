## Size of Wheels

The discussion
[About to order R1S - struggling with 22" vs 20" with snow in mind?](https://www.reddit.com/r/Rivian/comments/1e6zykm/about_to_order_r1s_struggling_with_22_vs_20_with/)
on Reddit
strongly suggest 20-inch wheels against 22-inch wheels.

        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td><strong>Aesthetics</strong></td>

        <tr>
            <td><strong>Durability (Wheels)</strong></td>

            <td><strong>Low</strong> (More vulnerable to damage)</td>
