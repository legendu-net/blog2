## Tips As Owner of Verizon Family Plan

It is suggested that you accepts a new member into a family plan
only if they already own a single-line Verizon account. 

1. It saves you tons of effort educating people,
  e.g., prices, deals, different plans, Verizon vs other phone carriers, etc.

2. A person with a single-line Verizon plan has already made their commitment.
  They likely have alrady purchased new phone and sort out trade-in,
  so there's minimum effort needed from the account owner.

3. Port in a line from another phone carrier 
  and dealing with purchase and trade-ins at the same can be complicated, time-consuming and error-prone.
  If not done carefully or if the customer representative is not skilled,
  it messes up things waste you days of time.
  On the contratry,
  an existing single-line account can initiate a transfer request into another Verizon account online
  and all needed from the owner of the in-bound account is to accept the request. 
  This is easy and less error-prone.
